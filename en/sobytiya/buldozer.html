Bulldozer

Event Open World Vesuvius · J3-1 Every 4 Hours

Event Activation

The Bulldozer is located in the Vesuvius area, in the quarry at the north-eastern sector of grid square J3-1. To activate the event, you must make an offering by depositing all the items from the list below. Once started, all players in the zone will receive a text notification.

The event becomes available again every 4 hours of real time (provided it was successfully completed).

Offering
×6 Spark Plug
×1 Short Threaded Pipe
×4 Copper Wire
×1 Insulated Wire
×1 Working Car Battery Charge > 49%

Event Phases

Consists of 4 phases, 5 minutes each. There is a 30-second break between phases. The number of enemies scales up: if there are more than 10 players in the event zone, +5 enemies are added for each additional player.

Final boss. Defeating it is the required condition to complete the event.

Rewards

Upon successful completion, 84,000 experience points are split among all participants proportionally to their kill scores. Each player beyond the first ten increases the total pool by +750 XP.

In-game currency and rewards for the Top 3 positions are automatically sent as a package to the storage of NPC Anton Poddubny.
