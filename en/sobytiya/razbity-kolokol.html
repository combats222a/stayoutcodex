Broken Bell

Open world event · every 4 hours

Event Start

The event is available every 4 hours of real time, provided the previous one was successfully completed. The Broken Bell is located at the Airport location in grid square [C3-1], near the old flooded church.
1
Find the Broken Bell

Reach grid square [C3-1] at the Airport location — near the old flooded church.

2
Make an Offering

Place the items from the table below into the bell to start the event.

3
Wait for the notification

As soon as the offering is accepted, all players in the event area will receive a text notification that it has begun.

Offering Requirements

Item Quantity
Old Ship Shackle × 4
Old Ship Nail × 1

Event Phases

The number of enemies scales with the number of participants. If there are more than 10 characters in the event area, +5 enemies are added to each wave for every additional player.

Every participant who deals damage receives points. Your current leaderboard position is displayed at the top of the screen.

Experience and Currency

Upon successful completion, 76,500 XP is distributed among participants proportionally to their scored points. For each participant beyond ten, the total pool increases by +850 XP. The currency reward is calculated based on the rank achieved and sent via mail to Seryoga the Shuttle.
