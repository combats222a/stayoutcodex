Access Requirements

The expedition is available to characters of level 85 and above, only as part of a squad of two or more people. Entry is through Kostya Mazay in the Lubech Outskirts location. Upon entry, each player is automatically granted the quests "Road through the swamps to Usov" and "Collect supplies for Usov". Re-entry as a leader resets daily at 07:00 server time (/time command).

Characters below level 85 can gain access if one of the factions has a sufficient amount of reputation:
  • "Mokrukha" — more than 7,500 reputation points
  • Black Market — more than 7,500 reputation points
  • "Usov" Village — more than 3,000 reputation points
