Zone Events

Dynamic trials of the Zone — start them, survive, and claim your reward

Events are launched by players through item offerings at altars — after which a wave of mutants begins in the local area. You must hold out for the allotted time. Top-3 participants by damage dealt receive item rewards, while 4th to 10th places get monetary compensation.
