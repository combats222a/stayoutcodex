Access Requirements

The expedition is available to characters of level 50 and above, or to those who have the "Hunting the Zero Specimen" quest, and only as part of a squad of two or more players. The entrance is located in the Big Cave area and is guarded by "Ravenhood" PMC mercenaries. Entry is managed by Tagir Shirkhan. Upon entering, every player automatically receives the quest "Expedition to Paukan's Lair". Re-entry as a squad leader resets at 07:00 server time (check via the /time command).

Expedition Stages

I
Ventilation Control Panel — Clearing the Hall
On your way to the ventilation control panel, fight your way through spiders and outcasts while gathering Glowing Mushroom × 30 (cannot be missed). Once you reach the control panel, pull the switch lever on the nearby support pillar to trigger a mini-event.

    II
    The Collapse — Gathering Explosives and Clearing the Ventilation Hall
    Deep in the tunnels, the squad will run into a cave-in. To clear it, you must gather resources: Dynamite Charge × 40 Fuse Cord × 12

    Head down the tunnel to the right of the cave-in; search the Electrical Panels to find the cords. Past the two locked doors lies the room with ventilation "shutters", filled with toxic fumes. Descend to the lower level and pull the lever on the concrete block — this will turn on the ventilation and start a mini-event.

    • III
    The Crossing — Gathering Dynamite and Blasting the Collapse
    After the mini-event, the door out of the "shutter" hall will unlock. Backtrack slightly and follow the new path leading to a deep chasm with a crossing mechanism.
    // CROSSING MECHANICS
    The crossing requires two participants: one rides inside the cradle, while the second rotates the winch handle.
    • In the middle of the chasm near the pillars — stop the cradle and eliminate 2 Blyaks and 2 Brutors on both sides.
    • Upon reaching the opposite side — quickly search the dynamite boxes.
    Return to the cave-in site. Interact with the wireline indicator to lay down the blasting circuit. Plant the dynamite and interact with the detonator handle. The blast clears the path and completes the second stage of the quest.
    IV
    The Final Hall — Battle with Paukan
    Proceed through the cleared path. Rest at the respawn point in the first chamber, then drop down through the opening to the lower level. In the main hall, start the gas generator — it clears the toxic fumes and triggers the mini-event. The generator is unstable and may fail; monitor its operation and restart it if it shuts down.
    Optional: If anyone in the squad has a Restored Head of the Vulcan Guardian × 1 — interact with the headless corpse by the wall and "attach" the head. This revives the Paukan Slayer, who will aid you in battle. The item cannot be recovered.

    • // "ELECTRO-FRY" MECHANICS — PAUKAN
    Paukan is terrified of electricity. To execute a shock attack, follow this cycle:
    • Activate the transformer switch (located to the right of the metal floor grid).
    • Activate the three switches on the Tesla coils along the perimeter of the hall — the transformer will overheat, cutting power to the grid, but the coils will remain active for 3 minutes.
    • While the coils are active — players standing near them are relatively safe. Deal maximum damage to Paukan.
    • One minute before the timer runs out — lure Paukan onto the metal grid and activate the laptop located there. This triggers a short circuit, causing Paukan to lose 1,000,000 HP.
    • To draw Paukan's attention — deal damage or break the cocoons in the center of the hall. The bait player should take cover behind the grid; Paukan will advance and retreat around it.
    • Important: Evacuate the grid position before the transformer restarts. Staying on the grid will result in instant death and completely destroy your body armor's durability.
    ⚠ The transformer can only withstand 3 short circuits. After the third, it will catch fire and permanently break down. Make your shots count.
    Defeating Paukan completes the third stage of the quest and immediately awards your loot.
    V
    Testing Laboratory — Bonus Stage
    Only accessible if the Restored Head of the Vulcan Guardian × 1 was used during Stage IV. The Paukan Slayer will break through a collapsed wall — pass through the gap to enter the lab.
    Interact with the testing facility doors to open them. Head up to the top floor, enter the control room, and flip the VT-1 Switch to begin the mini-event.
