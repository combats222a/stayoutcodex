Access Conditions

The expedition is available only to characters level 75 or higher, and only as part of a squad of two or more people. Entry is through Vostrikov on the Novaya Zemlya (Severny Island) location, after completing the "Abandoned Oil Platform" quest. Upon entering the location, every player is automatically given the "And the road in the ice again..." quest. Re-entry as a leader resets every day at 07:00 server time (command /time).

Expedition Stages

I
First Island — Clearing the Territory
Once on the pier, head to the southeastern sector of square D2-1. Enter the house with the sphere on the roof and talk to Dr. Miroslav Galo — a mini-event will begin.

  • Yeti × 15

⏱ 5 minutes — kill 171 enemies
  • Frozen Outcast × 50

  • White Rat × 70
Phase III
⏱ 7 minutes — kill 177 enemies
  • Frozen Outcast × 50
  • Polar Wolf × 25
  • Yeti × 20
  • Ice Slime × 20
  • White Rat × 65
  • Special Polar Bear × 1
II
Second Island — ATV and Cargo Delivery
After receiving the coordinates of the missing ATV, go to the helipad and talk to Antokha Sorokin — take the helicopter to the second island.

On the island, find the ATV. Boxes with equipment and provisions are scattered along the road — they are being attacked by mutants. Protect the boxes and load them onto the helicopter: no more than 6 boxes fit on one flight, so several trips will be needed.

On the oil platform: wooden equipment boxes → storage (pallet); paper food boxes → canteen (pallet around the corner to the right). After unloading, return for the remaining boxes. When everything is delivered — talk to Sator Tenet in the storage room.
III
Third Island — Bunker
After Sator Tenet's failed experiment, take the helicopter to the bunker on the third island. Find the blast door and enter. Activate the security system console — a mini-event will begin.
// MINI-EVENT · BREAK BETWEEN PHASES: 30 SEC
Phase I
⏱ 4 minutes — kill 122 enemies
  • Frozen Outcast × 40
  • White Rat × 70
  • Ice Slime × 10
  • Snow Imp × 5
Phase II
⏱ 5 min 30 sec — kill 191 enemies
  • Frozen Outcast × 65
  • White Rat × 85
  • Yeti × 20
  • Snow Imp × 10
  • Ice Slime × 15
After clearing, the blast door deeper into the bunker will open — go down into the caves.
IV
Caves Under the Bunker — Equipment Boxes
Before descending into the caves, be sure to visit the point at the entrance (condition for quest credit). In the caves, find 5 wooden equipment boxes, load them into the helicopter, and deliver them to the oil platform. Place the boxes on the second pallet in the storage room.
V
Oil Platform — Final Defense
After placing the last box, talk to Sator Tenet again (storage room, next to the pallet) — a mini-event will begin.
// MINI-EVENT · BREAK BETWEEN PHASES: 30 SEC
⚠ Every minute, one random valve on the platform will be damaged. You have ~60 seconds to tighten it. You need to tighten the valves a total of 10 times. Failure results in a phase reset.
Phase I
⏱ 6 minutes — kill 180 enemies
  • White Rat × 100
  • Polar Wolf × 20
  • Frozen Outcast × 70
Phase II
⏱ 7 min 30 sec — kill 238 enemies
  • Frozen Outcast × 55
  • Ice Slime × 15
  • White Rat × 180
  • Special Polar Bear × 1
Successful completion of all five stages means the quest is finished, and a reward is granted.
